How can you help?

There are multiple ways to donate in memory of Christina.

Proceeds from the concert will go toward The Christina Marie Cullen Memorial Scholarship at The University of the Arts for Photography. Additional proceeds will be donated towards breast cancer research and awareness in her name.



Christina Cullen Memorial Scholarship

This scholarship is awarded to an exceptional photography student at The University of The Arts. If you buy a ticket to the event (even if you can't make it), all proceeds go toward the scholarship fund.

Breast Cancer Research Foundation

Make a donation directly toward breast cancer research in Christina's name on the Breast Cancer Research Foundation's Time For Research website.

Susan G. Komen For The Cure

Make a donation in Christina's name to the Susan G. Komen Foundation.
