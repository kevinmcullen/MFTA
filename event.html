What we do

In an effort to bring together local artists and musicians, the Cullen family will host its 9th annual Music For The Arts on Jan. 16, 2016.

This event affords artists the opportunity to donate or display their work at the benefit while local musicians take the stage. If you are a photographer, painter, or any other kind of artist, this is a great opportunity to network and get your art out there for people to see.

Friends, family, newcomers and neighbors are invited to enjoy the sounds and take in some culture. $15 entrance fee/donation will be accepted at the door. All donations will be made in the name of our sister, daughter, cousin and friend to many — Christina Marie Cullen. She was a gifted artist, photographer and musician and this is the only true way to honor her life.

In addition, we are always looking for donations of all kinds — big or small — for our raffle and auction. It's a great way to represent your business or art with this local community gathering.

If you are interested in participating or donating, please contact Jason Cullen at jasoncullen78@gmail.com.

2017 LINEUP

The Griz Band

Loosely described as an Americana-Folk artist, Griz's work defies an easy pigeonhole as it effortlessly pushes the boundaries of compatible genres including reggae, rock, alternative and country, while still maintaining his unique and recognizable signature sounds.

Next Wednesday

Next Wednesday is a band of musical brethren whose sounds are influenced by the behemoths of rock and roll and nuanced by their passion for folk, blues, pop, and improvisational jams.

The TC Cole Riot

The Riot is a collective effort of musicians surrounding the songwriting of TC Cole, stylistically coming from Folk, Punk, Country and Blues music.

Droppin' Deuce

Christina's brothers, naturally, perform every year. Brian on the drums. Jason on the Bass. They are Droppin' Deuce.

HighKick

HighKick is a West Philadelphia based band who continue to evolve and challenge themselves as performers. Presenting their fans with improvisational jams, layered indie sagas, bluegrass singalongs and a trilogy of Christmas Musicals to boot.
